AI Prompt Examples For Writing

Click on a card to see examples of good AI prompts for each writing use case. These are designed to help students get the most out of AI for their writing assignments.

Essay Brainstorming
Get ideas and directions for your essay topic.

Suggest five unique thesis statements about the impact of social media on teenagers, each focusing on a different aspect (e.g., mental health, academic performance, social skills, activism, or misinformation).

What are some interesting angles for an essay on climate change solutions, considering economic, technological, political, and ethical perspectives?

Give me a list of possible arguments for and against school uniforms, including points about equity, self-expression, discipline, and school safety.

Brainstorm three essay topics about the role of artificial intelligence in education, and for each, suggest a possible research question.

Improving Clarity
Make your writing clearer and easier to understand.

Rewrite the following paragraph to make it clearer and more concise: [insert your paragraph here]. Also, highlight any sentences that could be confusing to a general audience.

Can you simplify this sentence for a 6th grade reader? [insert sentence]. Explain your changes.

Identify any jargon or complex language in this text and suggest simpler alternatives: [insert text].

Rephrase this technical explanation so that someone with no background in the subject can understand it: [insert explanation].

Grammar and Style Check
Check your writing for grammar, spelling, and style issues.

Check the following text for grammar and style issues, and suggest improvements for clarity and tone: [insert your text here].

Suggest improvements to make this paragraph more formal and academic: [insert paragraph].

Identify any passive voice in this passage and rewrite those sentences in active voice: [insert passage].

Review this email draft for professionalism and politeness: [insert email].

Generating Outlines
Organize your ideas with a structured outline.

Create a detailed outline for a research paper about renewable energy sources, including introduction, background, main arguments, counterarguments, and conclusion.

Make an outline for a persuasive essay on the benefits of reading fiction, with at least three main points and supporting evidence for each.

Generate an outline for a compare-and-contrast essay about online learning vs. in-person learning, including thesis statement and topic sentences for each section.

Outline a five-paragraph essay on the importance of community service for high school students, listing key points for each paragraph.

Paraphrasing
Restate sentences or paragraphs in different words.

Paraphrase the following sentence in simpler language and explain your word choices: [insert sentence here].

Rewrite this paragraph to avoid plagiarism, making sure to change both the wording and sentence structure: [insert paragraph].

Provide two different paraphrases of this statement, one formal and one informal: [insert statement].

Summarize this technical explanation in your own words for a non-expert audience: [insert explanation].

Citation Help
Generate citations in different styles.

Generate an MLA citation for this source: [insert source details here]. Also, provide a brief explanation of each part of the citation.

Provide an APA citation for this article: [insert article details]. Include both in-text and reference list formats.

Convert this Chicago-style citation to APA style: [insert citation].

List the information needed to create a proper citation for a website in MLA format.

Expanding Ideas
Develop a short idea into a full paragraph or section.

Expand on this idea to create a full paragraph, including an example and a transition to the next point: [insert idea here].

Add more detail to this argument by providing supporting evidence and addressing a possible counterargument: [insert argument].

Take this topic sentence and develop it into a complete body paragraph: [insert topic sentence].

Turn this list of bullet points into a coherent narrative paragraph: [insert bullet points].

Summarizing Text
Condense articles or essays into shorter summaries.

Summarize the following article in three sentences, focusing on the main argument and key evidence: [insert article here].

Give me a one-paragraph summary of this essay, highlighting the thesis and main supporting points: [insert essay].

Write a summary of this research paper for a general audience, avoiding technical jargon: [insert research paper].

Condense this chapter into a bullet-point list of the most important ideas: [insert chapter].

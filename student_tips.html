Student Guidance For AI Use

Click on a guideline card to reveal student‑focused tips on AI use that follow UW W‑course best practices.

Link to Your Course Context
Show how your assignment’s subject and audience shape your prompt.

I am a biology student writing a lab report on photosynthesis. Suggest three research questions I could investigate in my experiment.

Critical Thinking Example: After receiving AI suggestions, compare them to your course objectives and your own ideas. Identify which questions are most relevant and explain why you chose or modified them to better fit your assignment and audience.

Compose a summary for my economics 201 class explaining the main findings of a recent journal article on inflation, aimed at classmates unfamiliar with the topic.

As a history major, outline the key causes of the French Revolution in bullet points for a study guide.

I am a computer science student preparing a presentation on machine learning. What are three important points I should include for a technical audience?

Define Your Writing Objectives
Specify what you want to learn or demonstrate with your prompt.

Help me identify evidence for a persuasive essay on renewable energy, focusing on credible sources and statistics relevant to a university-level research paper.

Critical Thinking Example: When AI provides evidence, check each source for credibility and relevance. Reflect on how the evidence supports your thesis and whether you need to seek additional or more reliable sources to strengthen your argument.

Outline the steps to draft a literature review for a psychology course, including thesis development and source integration.

Provide an academic writing checklist so I can self‑assess structure, argument strength, and use of citations in my sociology essay.

List the learning objectives for a final project in a business communication course and suggest how to demonstrate them in my writing.

Break Down Tasks into Steps
Ask for iterative help—brainstorm, outline, draft, revise.

First, list five possible thesis statements for my essay on the impact of social media on college students' study habits.

Critical Thinking Example: After AI generates thesis statements, evaluate each for clarity, originality, and alignment with your assignment. Choose the best one and explain how you improved it using your own reasoning and course feedback.

Now that I have a thesis, create a detailed outline for a research paper in environmental science, including introduction, methods, results, and discussion.

Review this draft body paragraph from my engineering report and give suggestions to improve clarity and transitions.

Suggest a revision plan for my draft literature review in education, focusing on organization and integration of sources.

Ensure Inclusive, Fair Language
Craft prompts that avoid bias and honor diverse perspectives.

Suggest word choices to make this paragraph clear for non‑native English speakers without losing academic tone in a political science essay.

Critical Thinking Example: When AI suggests language changes, review for inclusivity and clarity. Reflect on whether the revised text is accessible to all readers and maintains the intended meaning and tone.

Revise this introduction to remove culturally biased language and maintain a respectful, neutral tone for a public health research paper.

Provide feedback on this argument to ensure it’s logically organized and avoids assumptions about different backgrounds in a philosophy assignment.

Review my group project report for inclusive language and suggest improvements for accessibility.

Explore Writing Styles & Conventions
Use AI to learn about writing styles, formats, and rhetorical conventions in your major.

What are the common rhetorical strategies used in psychology research articles at the undergraduate level?

Critical Thinking Example: After AI lists rhetorical strategies, compare them to examples from your course readings. Identify which strategies are most effective for your assignment and explain how you will apply or adapt them in your own writing.

Show me the typical structure of a biology lab report, including section headings and content expectations for a 200-level course.

How does persuasive writing in a business case study differ from persuasive writing in a humanities essay?

List the conventions for citing sources in an engineering technical report for a senior capstone project.

Break Down Assignments & Generate Outlines
Ask AI to analyze sample assignments or help you create practice outlines.

Analyze this assignment prompt for a sociology paper and list the key requirements: [paste assignment prompt]

Critical Thinking Example: After AI breaks down an assignment, check the requirements against your syllabus or rubric. Note any missing elements and explain how you will address them in your outline or draft.

Generate a detailed outline for a literature review in a psychology research methods course.

Break down the steps needed to complete a reflective essay for a nursing practicum.

What are the main components of a business case study, and how should I organize them for a management class?

Compare AI Writing to Published Examples
Compare AI-generated writing to instructor-provided or published examples in your field.

Generate a sample introduction for a chemistry lab report and compare it to this published example from my course reader: [paste example]

Critical Thinking Example: After comparing AI and published examples, identify strengths and weaknesses in each. Reflect on what you learned about effective writing in your field and how you will apply these lessons to your own work.

How does this AI-generated argumentative essay on climate policy differ from the one my professor provided in class?

List similarities and differences between this AI-written summary and a published article summary in a political science journal.

Evaluate the tone and structure of this AI-generated business memo versus a real company memo provided in my business writing course.

Explore Writing Strategies for Course Goals
Use AI to generate strategies that align with your course's writing objectives.

Suggest three different ways to phrase a thesis statement for my final paper in a political science course.

Critical Thinking Example: When AI suggests thesis statements or topic sentences, evaluate which best fits your assignment's goals and your own voice. Revise the suggestions and explain your reasoning for the changes you made.

Identify weak transitions between paragraphs in my engineering report and recommend improvements.

What are some strategies for integrating sources effectively in a literature review for a psychology class?

Generate topic sentences for each section of my business communication memo to ensure clarity and alignment with assignment goals.

Reflect on AI Use vs. Personal Revision
Consider how AI helped you meet writing goals and when your own revision was more effective.

How did using AI to generate an outline help me meet the assignment's learning objectives in my sociology course?

Critical Thinking Example: Reflect on how AI suggestions influenced your writing process. Did you accept, modify, or reject them? Explain how your choices supported your learning and improved your final draft.

Reflect on a time when my own revision improved my essay more than AI suggestions—what did I do differently?

Ask AI to compare its feedback on my draft to the feedback I received from my instructor. Where did they align or differ?

What are the benefits and limitations of using AI for revising a research proposal in a biology class?

Checklist for Writing Goals
Create a checklist for your writing goals and use AI to identify areas for improvement.

Generate a checklist for the writing goals of my final project in a public health course and use it to review my draft.

Critical Thinking Example: After using an AI-generated checklist, review your draft and identify areas where you met or missed your goals. Reflect on how you used the checklist to guide your revisions and learning.

Ask AI to identify which sections of my economics paper do not meet the assignment's stated objectives.

Provide a checklist for effective argumentation in a philosophy essay and highlight where my draft falls short.

Review my education research paper using this checklist and suggest specific improvements for each goal.

Use AI for Drafting & Expanding Ideas
Try AI to connect paragraphs or expand on underdeveloped sections during drafting.

Suggest a transition sentence to connect my introduction and first body paragraph in a psychology essay.

Critical Thinking Example: When AI suggests transitions or expansions, check if the new content fits your argument and style. Revise as needed and explain how your changes improved the flow and clarity of your writing.

Expand this underdeveloped idea in my engineering report to provide more detail and clarity: [paste idea]

How can I better connect the conclusion to the main arguments in my business case study?

Identify places in my draft where ideas are not fully explained and suggest ways to elaborate.

Use AI for Early Planning
Use AI to help outline topics, identify research questions, or draft a thesis before you start writing.

Generate an outline for a literature review in a biology course, including possible subtopics.

Critical Thinking Example: After using AI to plan your writing, review the outline for completeness and relevance. Add or adjust sections to better fit your assignment and explain your reasoning for any changes.

Suggest three research questions for a final project in a sociology class based on this topic: [insert topic]

Draft a thesis statement for my economics paper on the impact of minimum wage laws.

List possible angles or approaches for my education research proposal on student motivation.

Track and Reflect on AI Use
Keep a record of how you used AI and what changes you made to its suggestions.

After using AI to draft my introduction, list the changes I made to personalize the content and meet assignment requirements.

Critical Thinking Example: Keep a log of your AI use and reflect on which changes made your writing stronger. Explain how your revisions addressed assignment goals and improved your understanding of the topic.

Keep a log of each time I used AI for my research paper and describe how I revised the output to fit my own voice.

Reflect on which AI suggestions I accepted, modified, or rejected in my final draft and explain why.

Document how AI helped me brainstorm ideas and how I selected which ones to develop further in my own words.

Focus on Your Reasoning and Voice
Meet writing goals through your own thinking, even when using AI tools.

After receiving AI feedback, explain how I used my own reasoning to revise my thesis statement for a philosophy essay.

Critical Thinking Example: When revising AI-generated content, identify where your own reasoning or course knowledge led you to make changes. Reflect on how this process helped you develop your voice and meet assignment expectations.

Review the AI-generated introduction for my philosophy essay and revise it to better reflect my own perspective and course requirements.

How can I adapt this AI-suggested outline to fit my professor's expectations for a history research paper?

Summarize how I balanced AI-generated ideas with my own analysis in a business case study.

Use AI to Build Writing Skills
Use AI to learn strategies and improve your writing, not just as a shortcut.

Ask AI to explain why a particular transition is effective in connecting two paragraphs in my sociology essay.

Critical Thinking Example: After AI explains a writing strategy, try applying it to your own work. Reflect on what you learned and how you adapted the strategy to fit your assignment and learning goals.

Request feedback from AI on how to strengthen my topic sentences, then practice revising them myself.

Use AI to generate multiple ways to introduce a research question, then choose and adapt the one that fits my style best.

Reflect on what I learned about argument structure from AI suggestions and how I applied that knowledge to my own writing.

AI Prompt Examples For Writing

Click on a card to see examples of good AI prompts for each writing use case. These are designed to help students get the most out of AI for their writing assignments.

Essay Brainstorming
Get ideas and directions for your essay topic.

Suggest five unique thesis statements about the impact of social media on teenagers.

What are some interesting angles for an essay on climate change solutions?

Give me a list of possible arguments for and against school uniforms.

Improving Clarity
Make your writing clearer and easier to understand.

Rewrite the following paragraph to make it clearer and more concise: [insert your paragraph here]

Can you simplify this sentence for a 6th grade reader? [insert sentence]

Grammar and Style Check
Check your writing for grammar, spelling, and style issues.

Check the following text for grammar and style issues: [insert your text here]

Suggest improvements to make this paragraph more formal: [insert paragraph]

Generating Outlines
Organize your ideas with a structured outline.

Create a detailed outline for a research paper about renewable energy sources.

Make an outline for a persuasive essay on the benefits of reading fiction.

Paraphrasing
Restate sentences or paragraphs in different words.

Paraphrase the following sentence in simpler language: [insert sentence here]

Rewrite this paragraph to avoid plagiarism: [insert paragraph]

Citation Help
Generate citations in different styles.

Generate an MLA citation for this source: [insert source details here]

Provide an APA citation for this article: [insert article details]

Expanding Ideas
Develop a short idea into a full paragraph or section.

Expand on this idea to create a full paragraph: [insert idea here]

Add more detail to this argument: [insert argument]

Summarizing Text
Condense articles or essays into shorter summaries.

Summarize the following article in three sentences: [insert article here]

Give me a one-paragraph summary of this essay: [insert essay]
